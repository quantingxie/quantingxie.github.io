About Me

I'm Zhixuan Liu and

Hi! I am a fourth-year undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science from School of Data Science, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. I am also a research assistant and an upcoming master student at Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Dr. Jean Oh and PhD student Peter Schaldenbrand. My research interests lie in the intersection of machine learning and creating art, such as painting, video generation, and auto music creation.


Personally, I am a piano player with 15 years of performance experience, and I play with guitar and synthesizer in my spare time as well. I am also a Chinese traditional dancer with two gold awards from Beijing in 2010 and Hong Kong in 2011, and I'm practicing KPOP dance recently. Moreover, I really enjoy taking photos, especially film photography.
